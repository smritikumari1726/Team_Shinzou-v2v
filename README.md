# 🌸 ForHer

> Empowering women through fair careers and financial freedom using AI.

## 📌 Overview

ForHer is an AI-powered platform designed to help women at two important stages of their professional journey:

- **WorkEquity** – Detects gender bias in job descriptions and suggests inclusive alternatives.
- **HerFinance** – Simplifies government loan and grant schemes for women entrepreneurs using a multilingual AI assistant.

Our mission is to make opportunities more transparent, accessible, and inclusive for every woman.

---

## ✨ Features

### 💼 WorkEquity
- Analyze job descriptions for gender bias
- AI-generated Fairness Score
- Highlights biased words and phrases
- Explains why the language may be biased
- Suggests an inclusive version of the job description
- Download and share fairness reports

### 💰 HerFinance
- AI chatbot for government schemes
- Multilingual support
- Personalized scheme recommendations
- Eligibility checker
- Required documents
- Step-by-step application guidance
- Simple explanations in easy-to-understand language

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many women struggle to find unbiased workplaces and access government financial schemes due to scattered information, complex language, and lack of transparency. Existing resources are often difficult to understand, causing many eligible women to miss valuable opportunities.

---

## 💡 Solution

ForHer bridges this information gap by using AI to:

- Promote fair and inclusive hiring practices.
- Help women understand and access financial support without confusion.
- Provide information in simple, conversational language.
- Support women throughout their career and entrepreneurial journey.
